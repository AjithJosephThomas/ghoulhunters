Step 1. A reporter spots an invasive species and takes a photo using the Nature Ninjas mobile app.

Step 2. The app sends the picture with location, species tag, timestamp, and any remarks to our proposed web application.

Step 3. The web app uses machine learning to check whether the sighting looks accurate.

Step 4. When several reports cluster together, the system alerts a Nature Ninjas volunteer nearby.

Step 5. The volunteer visits the site and collects specimens in a secure package — without spreading pests.

Step 6. The volunteer disposes material following Queensland rules or hands it to the biosecurity team.

Step 7. The Nature Ninjas app keeps tracking reports and statistics so everyone can see progress.

Step 8. Active reporters with accurate sightings earn thank-yous through our spotter rewards program.
