About us

Nature Ninjas is a Grade 5 community project from St Anthony’s Primary School in Kedron. We run invasive species watch programs across our region and help neighbours protect nature and biodiversity by sharing what to look for and how to report safely.

We are Nature Ninjas — kids and neighbours who use our ninja skills to spot invasive pests, report safely, and help experts keep Queensland’s environment healthy.

Why we care

Local rivers and habitats are part of our home. Invasive species can hurt native animals, plants, and the whole food web. We want everyone to know what to do — and what not to do — so we can be guardians of our environment.
