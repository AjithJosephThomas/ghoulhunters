Protecting our waterways

Invasive species threaten biodiversity, river health, and the places we love. Ghoulhunters helps kids, families, and neighbours learn, spot, and report safely — without touching or moving pests. When you tell us what you saw, experts can remove reported pests the right way and help our rivers recover. Active spotters who share genuine, accurate sightings can earn thank-you rewards — mugs, jackets, coupons, and more.
