Animated story: freshwater gold clams traveled on ship hulls to Australia, spread in the Brisbane River, and reduced oxygen which harms plants and fish. This is why biosecurity matters.

Step 1: Tiny freshwater gold clams hide on ship hulls and travel across the ocean.

Step 2: They arrive in Australia — a new home far from where they belong.

Step 3: The clams spread in the Brisbane River and grow in huge numbers.

Step 4: They use up oxygen. Plants and fish struggle — that is why biosecurity matters.
